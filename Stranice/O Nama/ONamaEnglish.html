Who are we?

Welcome to our Chinese restaurant, located in the heart of Belgrade at Terazije, with a tradition spanning over 50 years. Our story begins back in 1974 when we opened the doors of the first authentic Chinese restaurant in the city. Since then, we have continuously brought the taste of China to your plates.

Picture of our first location

Our chefs are masters of their craft, with years of experience in preparing traditional dishes such as Peking duck, Kung Pao chicken, and Szechuan beef. Additionally, we offer vegetarian options that will satisfy every guest.

Visit us and enjoy a warm ambiance, friendly service, and unforgettable flavors. Our mission is to provide you with an unforgettable culinary experience that you will want to repeat. Thank you for being a part of our story that has lasted for half a century.
